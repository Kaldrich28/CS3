What is Ukelele

The ukulele, also called Uke, is a member of the lute family of instruments of Portuguese origin and popularized in Hawaii.
It generally employs four nylon strings. The tone and volume of the instrument vary with size and construction.
Ukuleles commonly come in four sizes: soprano, concert, tenor, and baritone.
